About EGLS

Get to know us better...

Exceeding Glory Learning System Ltd is an educational firm established in 2017 for sales of books and teacher training. Egls offers sales of quality foreign and local books that aid learning as specified in the British, American and Nigeria curriculum.
Our training center is well equipped to deliver long and short courses on Montessori Education, Handwriting and design, Jolly Phonics, Classroom Management, School Administrative seminars. The Executive Director is an astute reader and a leader in different facets of life.
